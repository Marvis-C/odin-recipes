Linguine with broccoli rabe and Sicilian red prawns

Return to Main Directory

Ingredients

  • 380g of linquine
  • 500g of broccoli rabe
  • 20g red prawns, shelled and cleaned
  • 2 garlic cloves,finely chopped
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • salt
  • pepper

Method

  1. Wash and chop the broccoli rabe and set aside
  2. In a frying pan, soften the garlic in a little oil and add the broccoli rabe, along with a ladle of the cooking water from the linguine
  3. Drain the pasta and mix with the broccoli rabe. Stir well and add the prawns at the end, just to lightly cook through. Season with salt and pepper
  4. Arrange neatly on a serving plate and finish with a sprinkle of grated lemon zest
